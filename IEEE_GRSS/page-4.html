Who We Are

Our History

Founded at Stanford University, our chapter connects students with global innovations in remote sensing, Earth science, and interdisciplinary tech.

We've grown from a small group of passionate researchers to a thriving community of over 100 members, all united by our interest in geoscience and remote sensing technology.
